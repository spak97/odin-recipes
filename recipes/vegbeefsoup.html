Vegetable Beef Soup

Description

Simple! Brown the meat, and throw it all together into one pot. Serve soup with a cake of corn bread or grilled cheese sandwiches on a cold winter night. You will feel warm all over.

Ingredients

  • Lean ground beef
  • 4 cans mixed veggies
  • 4 cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 chopped onion
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. In a large soup pot, cook ground meat over medium heat until browned. Drain grease from the pot.
  2. Add chopped onion, mixed vegetables, and tomatoes. Give it a stir. Reduce heat, and simmer for about 3 to 4 hours. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
