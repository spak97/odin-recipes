Instant Ramen

Description

Classic spicy korean instant ramen that is extremely easy to cook and enjoy especially on cold, rainy days.

Ingredients

  • Korean instant ramen
  • Green onions
  • Thinly sliced beef
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Follow the steps on the wrapper of the ramen
  2. Add in the egg and sliced beef after 2-3 mins of noodle cooking
  3. Add in chopped green onions at the very end and enjoy!
